Terminal CSS

Turn any plain old html document and make it look like a snazy terminal!
Inspired to make this by a certain somebody who loves the terminal (you know who you are!) I've made this simple fun repo for people to use :)

I've managed to include all the classic stuff from the terminal including:

You can be very bold!!! or you can shy away and dim. Typical link looking underlined and the old classic BLINK!!! Want a change? Try reverse! Feel free to explore just don't hide away!
